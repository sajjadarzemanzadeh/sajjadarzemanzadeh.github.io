About

I am a Research Assistant in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the University of Tehran. I received my bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering with a GPA of 16.62/20 (last two years GPA: 17.52/20) from University of Tehran, and I was fortunate to be advised by Dr. Ehsan Hosseinian. During my undergraduate study, my main focus was on compound effect of shear and squeeze-film dissipation in a silicon lateral micro-resonator for MEMS-based environmental monitoring applications. Currently, I am a last-year master's student majoring in Biomechanics with a GPA of 17.91/20, and I am thrilled to work with Dr. Ehsan Hosseinian and Dr. Maryam Mahnama, and be a part of Multiscale Simulation of Materials and Structures (MSMS) Lab for the past two year. For my thesis, I am working on multiscale modeling of particle reinforced PNIPAM hydrogel to create a tissue-like biomimetic material. I am interested in computational mechanics and multiscale modeling, biomimetic materials, machine learning, and optimization.
